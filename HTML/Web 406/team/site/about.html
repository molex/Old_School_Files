About Us 

Doodle Coffee stocks are discriminately selected from the Worlds Finest Coffee plantations. All Coffees are of the highest grades available, we do not carry second grade Coffees. All Organic Coffee stocks have been certified under strict guidelines.

With a wide selection of the world's finest gourmet coffee offerings, we cup test each origin to ensure it is the "best of class." We use a delicious Colombian Supremo base for our flavored coffees, leaving you with a one-stop shop for all your specialty green or roasted beans.

At Doodle Coffee Company, we take pride in making sure that each bag of coffee you purchase will be of excellent quality and freshness. We select only 100% Arabica coffee beans that are hand-picked and tested for quality before we buy them from the growers. No matter how exotic the location (Central and South America, East Africa and Arabia, Indonesia and the Pacific) we seek only the finest crop offerings from the top growing regions in the world, and Doodle Coffee Company supports sustainability by using certified Organics, Rainforest and Fair Trade coffee whenever possible.

We test every batch for quality after we roast it, and this ensures that you are getting the best tasting, top quality coffee, at affordable prices. We roast each variety of coffee bean the old- fashioned way— by hand, and in small batches. This allows us full control of the roasting process. After roasting the coffee beans, we immediately package and vacuum seal each batch of gourmet coffee to guarantee its freshness. Upon taking your first sip of your favorite Doodle coffee, you will immediately notice the care we take to assure that you are getting the best product we can deliver: coffee perfection!
