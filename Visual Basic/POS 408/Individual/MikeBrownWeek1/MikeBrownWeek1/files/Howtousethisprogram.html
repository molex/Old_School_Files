How to use this program

The Salary Calculator is a very simple program designed to take a few employee names and calculate how much they will get paid for a given week. This program has headings for the Employee name, their pay rate, the hours worked, and the weekly pay.


The employee name and pay rate are already entered for you. All you need to do is enter the number of hours that each employee has worked for a given week (anything over 40 hours will generate overtime. Overtime is shown in a green font).


Once you have entered the number of hours for each employee (if an employee has not worked this week, enter 0 [zero]. DO Not Leave it Blank!!!), you may click on the Calculate button (or press the enter key). This will tabulate the weekly pay for each employee and will display the amount in the label under the Weekly Pay heading.


If you have made a mistake, you may click on the "Clear" button. This will clear out all data that you have entered.


When you have finished, you may click on the exit button.



Thanks, and enjoy the program :)
